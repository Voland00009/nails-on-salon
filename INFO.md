## 7. Brand Voice & Description

### Technique (About / Services page)

I specialize in a gentle dry technique that creates a neat, refined appearance and allows the manicure to grow out cleanly. This method helps maintain a well-groomed look for up to 4–5 weeks with minimal maintenance. The focus is on precision, smooth finish, and a naturally elegant result.

### Products & Materials

In my work, I use hard gel systems that are more flexible and nail-friendly, helping to protect the natural nail while providing strength and durability. This allows for a lightweight feel with long-lasting results compared to more aggressive materials.

### Service Descriptions (long-form, for Services page)

**Gel Overlay (Dry Manicure)**
A structured dry manicure designed for a clean, refined, and long-lasting result. This service includes detailed cuticle care, precise nail shaping, and gel leveling to create a smooth, even surface. The goal is a natural, polished look that grows out beautifully and stays neat for weeks.

**Nail Extensions (Upper Forms / Hard Gel)**
Extensions are created using upper forms and hard gel to build length and shape while keeping the nails lightweight and comfortable. I work with high-quality, low-toxicity hard gel systems that are gentle on the natural nail and provide strong, flexible wear.

**Smart Touch Dry Pedicure**
A dry pedicure focused on precise foot care and a clean aesthetic result. This service includes careful treatment of the skin and soles, helping achieve smooth, well-groomed feet with a natural, fresh appearance.

### Additional context (from Instagram)
- French pedicure: "a flawless style that combines tenderness and sophistication, adding elegance"
- Warm, feminine aesthetic — consistent across studio décor and photography
